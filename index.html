The Challenge

What is the challenge?

The challenge is to use either a Concept2 rowing machine, Concept2 SkiErg or Concept2 Bike Erg to cover 30 half marathons on the Rower or SkiErg OR 30 full marathons on the Bike Erg.


When does the challenge take place?

The challenge starts at 00:00 on the 10th of November and continues until 23:59 on the 24th of December in your own time zone


Total metres

Since the beginning of the challenge everyone who has taken part has managed to accumulate 97,130,588 metres. Thats 60,354 Miles
